## Overview Unleash the power of visual exploration with our cutting-edge Image Retrieval App seamlessly combining advanced image processing techniques with intuitive user interfaces, our app offers two distinctive functionalities: ### 1. Text-Based Image Retrieval: Describe the image you have in mind, and let our app find the closest match for you. From fashion styles to scenic landscapes, explore a world of visuals based on your text input. ### 2. Image-Based Similarity Search: Upload an image and our app will find three other images that share similarities. Ideal for discovering variations of your favorite visuals or exploring related content.
